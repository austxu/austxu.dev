# Third-party notices

## World map data and generation

The generated world-map assets in `public/` use land geometry from
[Natural Earth](https://www.naturalearthdata.com/), which places its map data
in the public domain.

The geometry is distributed by
[`world-atlas`](https://github.com/topojson/world-atlas) under the ISC License.
The generation script also uses
[`topojson-client`](https://github.com/topojson/topojson-client), also under the
ISC License. Their license texts are included with their installed packages.
